# Decision Log

## 2026-06-25

Decision: Use seven total agents.

Structure:

- One Project Director
- One Product Lead
- Two sub-agents under the Product Lead
- One Engineering Lead
- Two sub-agents under the Engineering Lead

Rationale:

- The user only needs to receive manager-agent reports.
- Product and Engineering are separated so product judgment and implementation judgment do not get mixed.
- The number of sub-agents stays small enough to reduce management cost.

Deferred:

- Automating external customer contact
- Automating real deployment
- Automating device-control features
